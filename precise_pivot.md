## 1. The POV Pivot: From Privacy Infrastructure to Agency Control & Optimization

**Original POV (from your initial overview)**:  
Precise.ai was designed as a privacy-preserving data intelligence platform for brands, agencies, and data providers. It focused on secure collaboration without sharing raw PII — using federated clean rooms, identity graphs, and Valence cryptographic proofs to enable look-alike modeling, audience activation, and closed-loop measurement. The core thesis: "Verification + Intelligence" to build trust in a post-cookie world, with integrations like MadHive and The Trade Desk for activation. The platform emphasized data ownership, proof-based advertising, and interoperability, solving jobs-to-be-done like brand look-alike modeling and closed-loop measurement.

**New POV (refined from our discussions)**:  
The market is demanding immediate control over programmatic waste more than abstract privacy tools. Agencies are losing 35–47% of data CPM to auto-appended segments they never requested, with no visibility or proof of value. Precise.ai pivots to become the neutral layer that gives agencies real-time veto power, automated optimization, and unbreakable proof — moving more budgets into working media while empowering local teams to pull national dollars. This includes offering better, provenance-backed data from our network that has proven outcomes, replacing waste with segments that deliver verifiable lift.

This isn't a full rebuild: 80–90% of our existing tech (Valence proofs, identity resolution, federated compute, activation connectors) directly powers the new story. We shift from selling to brands/CDOs (possession-focused) to agencies/traders (performance-focused), starting with MadHive as the wedge. CDPs are a natural customer segment, where we act as a "filter" for ad-tech activations to ensure clean audiences don't get wasted post-export.

**Why this pivot now?**  
- Real pilots (as you confirmed) show 35–47% waste savings and 29–41% ROAS lift, proving agencies will pay for control and proof.  
- The original doc highlights agency pain: "I need to prove our digital ad spend is driving sales, but connecting data is a privacy nightmare." This pivot directly addresses that by focusing on waste elimination and outcome-proven data.  
- Public market data (e.g., ANA Q2 2025) aligns with $26.8B in programmatic waste, where data inefficiencies eat 35–50% of CPMs from redundancy/overlap in 40+ appended segments.
